About Me

Hello! I'm Sanjeev Ladwal, a passionate and driven student currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Engineering (B.E.) in the field of Information Technology at MBM University Jodhpur, Rajasthan. My journey began in 2022 and I am set to complete my degree in 2026.

Originally from the beautiful Kathua District, Jakhbar Village in Jammu and Kashmir, I have always been fascinated by technology and its potential to transform the world. Over the years, I have developed a strong interest in computer programming and web development.

My skill set includes languages like: C, C++, Python, and I specialize in Front-End Web Development. I am passionate about creating user-friendly websites and applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various frameworks.

I am constantly learning new technologies and improving my skills to stay at the forefront of the industry. I believe in the power of innovation and aim to solve real-world problems through creative software solutions.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring new ideas, reading tech blogs, and participating in coding challenges to test my problem-solving abilities. My goal is to make meaningful contributions to the tech community and build tools that make life easier.
